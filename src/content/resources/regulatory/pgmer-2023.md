## Overview

The Postgraduate Medical Education Regulations 2023 (PGMER-2023) issued by the National Medical Commission govern all postgraduate medical training (MD, MS, DM, MCh, diploma) in India. They replace the earlier MCI regulations and introduce significant digital assessment requirements.

## Key Requirements

- Mandatory **e-logbook** maintenance by all PG trainees
- Competency-based training with defined milestones
- Workplace-based assessment as a core evaluation method
- Regular mentor-mentee interactions with documented feedback
- Thesis/dissertation requirement with documented progress

## Implications for Medical Colleges

Institutions must provide digital infrastructure for e-logbook maintenance, competency tracking, and assessment documentation. Paper logbooks are no longer sufficient for regulatory compliance.

## How ePortfolios Help

ePortfolios serve as the comprehensive digital platform for e-logbook compliance — journals for logging clinical encounters, plans for tracking procedures, SmartEvidence for competency mapping, and submissions for structured assessment workflows.
