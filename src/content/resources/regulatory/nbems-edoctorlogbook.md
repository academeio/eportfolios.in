## Overview

The National Board of Examinations in Medical Sciences (NBEMS) launched the eDoctorLogbook as a mandatory digital logbook for all DNB, DrNB, and FNB trainees. This is a separate system from NMC's e-logbook requirements and applies specifically to NBEMS-accredited training programmes.

## Key Requirements

- All DNB/DrNB/FNB trainees must maintain their logbook on the eDoctorLogbook platform
- Regular entries documenting clinical procedures, case discussions, and training activities
- Supervisor verification of logged activities
- Logbook completion is a prerequisite for appearing in final examinations

## Implications for Medical Colleges

Institutions running NBEMS-accredited programmes must ensure trainee compliance with eDoctorLogbook. This is a mandated platform — institutions cannot substitute their own systems for NBEMS logbook requirements.

## How ePortfolios Help

While eDoctorLogbook handles the NBEMS-mandated logbook requirement, ePortfolios complement it by providing deeper competency tracking, reflective practice, EPA assessment, and mentor feedback that the logbook platform does not cover. Institutions can use both systems — eDoctorLogbook for NBEMS compliance and ePortfolios for comprehensive CBME implementation.
