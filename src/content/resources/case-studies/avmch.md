## Institution Profile

- **Name:** Arupadai Veedu Medical College and Hospital (AVMCH), Pondicherry
- **Type:** Medical college and teaching hospital
- **Programmes:** Postgraduate residency training
- **Platform:** Academe ePortfolios on Academe Cloud

## Challenge

AVMCH needed a digital platform to support PG residency training documentation, competency tracking, and assessment workflows aligned with evolving NMC requirements.

## Implementation

Deployed Academe ePortfolios as a managed service on Academe Cloud, providing residents and faculty with tools for clinical encounter logging, competency tracking, and structured assessment.

## Results

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Users | 710 |
| Active since | 2021 |

## Lessons Learned

1. **Managed service reduces friction** — turnkey deployment on Academe Cloud eliminated IT infrastructure concerns
2. **Smaller institutions benefit equally** — ePortfolio value is not limited to large deemed universities
3. **Adoption follows integration** — embedding ePortfolios into mandatory training workflows drives consistent usage
