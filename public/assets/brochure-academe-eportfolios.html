The challenge

Paper logbooks cannot deliver what CBME demands

India's 706+ medical colleges face a regulatory mandate with no adequate tools. NMC requires competency-based training and e-logbooks. NAAC demands outcome-based documentation. Most institutions still rely on paper.

1

NMC mandates e-logbooks

PGMER-2023 requires digital logbooks for PG trainees. Paper logbooks provide no analytics, no longitudinal tracking, and no evidence mapping.

2

Competency is longitudinal

EPAs require multiple workplace-based assessments over months. Paper cannot aggregate or visualise trends across dozens of assessments.

3

No Indian platform exists

Global platforms cost USD 15–50/user/year and align with ACGME, not NMC. India needs an affordable, regulation-aware solution.

The solution

Academe ePortfolios

An open-source ePortfolio platform purpose-built for competency-based medical education in India. Delivered as a turnkey managed service on Academe Cloud with affordable per-user pricing.
